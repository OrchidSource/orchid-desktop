About ORCHID

Welcome to a world where users own the internet.

The Orchid protocol is the decentralized, open-source technology for an Internet free from surveillance and censorship.

Orchid's open source technology protocol combines surplus bandwidth, state-of-the-art encryption, and a decentralized infrastructure built so that people on all corners of the globe can participate and exchange bandwidth for payment in peer-to-peer transactions using Orchid tokens on the Ethereum blockchain.

The Orchid Network will evolve so that the millions of users and nodes distributed globally will fortify the network against attacks.

As the greatest tool for human communication and collaboration ever invented, Orchid Labs believes that the Internet should be open, accessible, and censorship-free across the globe for every single user.
